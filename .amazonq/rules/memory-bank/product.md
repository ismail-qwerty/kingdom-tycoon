# Product Overview

## Project Purpose
Kingdom Tycoon is an idle clicker/tycoon game for Android where players build and expand a legendary kingdom through strategic resource management, progression systems, and prestige mechanics. The game combines tap-to-earn mechanics with automated income generation, creating an engaging incremental gameplay loop.

## Value Proposition
- **Progressive Era System**: Players advance through 5 distinct historical eras (Medieval → Legendary), each with unique buildings and visual themes
- **Deep Prestige Mechanics**: 3-tier prestige system (Ascension, Rift, Legend) provides permanent bonuses and meta-progression
- **Parallel Dimension**: Shadow Kingdom offers doubled income opportunities with strategic risk/reward gameplay
- **Hero Collection**: 12 legendary heroes with unique passive abilities that enhance kingdom growth
- **Idle Optimization**: Offline earnings system allows progress even when not actively playing

## Key Features

### Core Gameplay
- **Tap Mechanics**: Direct player interaction to earn gold through tapping
- **Building System**: 25 unique buildings across 5 eras with exponential cost/income scaling
- **Automated Income**: Buildings generate passive gold per second
- **Advisor System**: Automates building purchases and resource management

### Progression Systems
- **Era Progression**: Unlock new eras by reaching gold thresholds
- **Prestige System**: Reset progress for Crown Shards that provide permanent multipliers
- **Achievement System**: 50 achievements across 10 categories with reward bonuses
- **Quest System**: Dynamic objectives that guide player progression

### Advanced Features
- **Shadow Kingdom**: Parallel dimension with 2x income multiplier
- **Map Exploration**: Isometric map with explorable tiles and rewards
- **Raid System**: Combat encounters for additional resources
- **Weekly Events**: Time-limited challenges with special rewards
- **Hall of Legends**: Prestige tracking and milestone rewards

### Monetization
- **AdMob Integration**: Rewarded video ads for gameplay boosts
  - Double offline earnings
  - Speed boost multipliers
  - Era transition interstitials
  - Map screen banners

### Technical Features
- **Save System**: Automatic save every 30 seconds with anti-cheat validation
- **Offline Earnings**: Calculates income earned while away (24-hour cap)
- **Performance Optimization**: Targets 60 FPS on mid-range Android devices
- **Statistics Tracking**: Comprehensive lifetime stats for player engagement

## Target Users
- **Casual Mobile Gamers**: Players who enjoy idle/incremental games with minimal time commitment
- **Strategy Enthusiasts**: Players who optimize builds and prestige timing
- **Collectors**: Players motivated by unlocking all heroes and achievements
- **Progression-Focused**: Players who enjoy long-term meta-progression systems

## Use Cases
- **Short Sessions**: Quick tap sessions during breaks (1-5 minutes)
- **Active Play**: Engaged building management and optimization (15-30 minutes)
- **Idle Progress**: Passive income generation while away from game
- **Long-Term Goals**: Multi-week progression toward prestige milestones and era unlocks
